Social Event

We’re happy to announce that this year’s social dinner will take place on August 31st (Wednesday), from 18:30 to 20:30 at Sandoase.

Sandoase is a cosy beach club next to the Rhine river at the corner of the three countries with a beautiful view of the river and sunset.

Address:

Westquaistrasse 75,

4057 Basel, Switzerland

 

Social event is for the EuroSciPy2022 attendees only. Attendees can buy additional tickets to bring along partners and friends. Please bring your badge to the event.

Please note that there are a limited number of places available. The ticket sale starts on Monday 22nd August and the price includes a meal of your choice and a drink.

You can buy your ticket here:

Get Social Event Ticket.

We’re looking forward to a wonderful evening with you!

In case of any questions, please contact us at: info@euroscipy.org.
